The movie was about Kasparov won the IBM Deep Blue with the score 4-2 in six matches in 1996; however, after IBM upgraded its Deep Blue computer in 1997 with the ability to calculate 200 million steps per second, Kasparov became the first chess champion to fail in a machine. In other words, my interest in IT is web developer at the moment.
